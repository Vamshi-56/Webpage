Stay Vigilant in the Digital Era

Every click online is a doorway — some lead to knowledge, others to risk. Cybercrime is real, personal, and growing rapidly.

India ranks among the top 5 countries for online fraud in 2025. Be aware. Be alert.

🔗 Phishing Attacks

82% of users fall for fake banking links, mimicking official websites.

Case: Hyderabad woman lost ₹8.6L via fake LIC email.

💬 OTP Scams

Fraudsters pose as KYC agents and steal OTPs through fake calls.

Case: Bengaluru youth lost ₹1.2L updating “SIM KYC”.

🖥️ Remote Access Apps

Apps like AnyDesk let attackers remotely control your device when misused.

Tip: Never install apps shared via unknown links.

🔒 Cyber Safety Tips

  • Never share your OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone—even bank officials.
  • Use multi-factor authentication wherever possible.
  • Install apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
  • Always double-check the URL before entering credentials.
  • Report suspicious emails or messages to cybercrime.gov.in.
